Design Brief

This 8-week module teaches students to design a health, fitness, or wellness app using user-centred design and insights from AI and machine learning. It covers user research, competitor analysis, and creating personalised, intelligent experiences.

Concept

Eddy is combines fitness and community for surfers. Users can share sessions, track progress, and connect. AI-driven training plans and surf spot recommendations adapt to user skill and goals through Machine Learning
